Chicken Primavera

A light and easy dish to make at home with last of your veg.

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • fresh vegetables such as, carrot, onion, peppers, tomato, garlic.
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 teaspoons of italian seasoning
  • 2 table spoons of lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese

Method

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil, add salt, pasta and cook according to package instructions
  2. While pasta is boiling, start to cook your veg so that cooking will finish about the same time.
  3. Heat olive oil in a deep pan over a medium heat.
    4. Add red onion, and carrots and saute for 2 minutes.
    Add peppers and tomatoes and saute for 2 more minutes. Finally add garlic to the pan and saute for 2 more minutes.
  4. Once pasta is cooked, drain and add into the pan with the vegetables. Add lemon juice and season to taste.
    Add the parmesan cheese and stir
